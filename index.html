Form W-4 (2025)
Employee's Withholding Certificate · IRS

Tells the firm how much federal income tax to withhold from your paycheck. Complete Steps 1 and 5 at minimum.

⬇ Download Blank W-4 (IRS.gov) ☁ Upload Completed W-4 to OneDrive
Quick Tips:
• Step 1: Enter your name, address, SSN, and filing status.
• Step 2: Only complete if you hold more than one job or your spouse also works.
• Step 3: Enter qualifying child/dependent credits if applicable.
• Step 5: Sign and date — required for the form to be valid.
🪪
Form I-9 (2026)
Employment Eligibility Verification · USCIS

Verifies your identity and right to work in the U.S. You complete Section 1; the firm completes Section 2 within 3 business days of your start date. Bring originals on Day 1.

⬇ Download Blank I-9 (USCIS.gov) ☁ Upload Completed I-9 to OneDrive
List A (any one): U.S. Passport, Permanent Resident Card, Employment Auth. Document.

List B + C (one from each): Driver's license plus Social Security card or certified birth certificate.

Bring original documents on Day 1 for the office administrator to complete Section 2.
